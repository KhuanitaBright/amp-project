Tailor-Made Sectional Title Insurance for Body Corporates covers the following:
  • Public Supply Connections & Rent
    • Cover for accidental damage to installations and connections to the properties for which the body corporate is legally responsible such as, Gas, water, sewerage and telecommunications connections.
  • Accidental Damage And Office Contents
    • Loss of or damage to contents of the office, (excluding electronic data processing equipment), foyer, clubhouse, gatehouse, reception and laundry, belonging to the body corporate or for which the body corporate is responsible.
    • Cover is basic perils plus Theft, Accidental Breakage of Glass and Malicious Damage.
    • Cover is subject to average and burglar alarm warranty and first amount payable
    • Loss of Documents may be covered.
    • Claims Preparation Costs are included
  • Public Liability
    • Third Party Property Damage or Bodily Injury/Illness arising from the Insured’s ownership of the Insured property
    • Retroactive Date the inception date when the Policy was first incepted with Constantia Insurance Company Limited
    • Property owners liability
    • Wrongful Arrest & Defamation
    • Legal Defence Costs
  • Trustees’ Liability
    • Liability for payment of VAT
    • Remuneration for which the Body Corporate and/or trustees are legally liable
    • Indemnity claimable by any other more specific insurance in force
    • Circumstances known to trustees and/or body corporate prior to inception of this Policy
    • Claims for death, bodily injury, sickness, disease or damage to property
    • Libel, slander or other defamatory or disparaging material
    • Any trustee(s) gaining any personal profit or advantage to which they were not legally entitled
    • Any act committed by a trustee which is criminal, illegal, fraudulent, dishonest or of malicious intent
    • Fines, penalties, exemplary, vindictive or aggravated damages
    • Conflict of interest
  • Employers Liability & All Risk
    • Covers gardening and maintenance equipment belonging to or used for the purposes of the body corporate
    • All items attract a premium applied to the values required
    • Gardening & Other Maintenance related Equipment
    • Gate Motors
    • Specified Items at required rate
  • Money
    • Major Limit/Crossed Cheques limit
    • Receptacles & Clothing
    • Locks and Keys
    • Money on premises in excess of R1 500
    • Higher limits may be available at an agreed additional premium
    • Money in Transit Warranty
    • Personal Accident Assault Extension is available
  • Fidelity Guarantee
    • Covers loss of money and/or other property belonging to the Insured or for which they are responsible, stolen by a trustee or an employee/insurable person of the body corporate for dishonest personal financial gain
  • Machinery Breakdown
    • Swimming pools, saunas, spa baths, jacuzzis, automatic gates, garage doors, escalators, lifts, borehole pumps, Air-conditioning plant, hoists, transformers and electrical switchgear
  • Electronic Equipment
    • Electronic data processing equipment is limited to computers and all related hardware, peripherals and computer software and the information or data stored therein or thereon
    • Fire brigade charges
    • Hire purchase/finance agreements
    • Power surge extension
  • MR&R Warranty
  • Fidelity Guarantee
    • Covers loss of money and/or other property belonging to the Insured or for which they are responsible, stolen by a trustee or an employee/insurable person of the body corporate for dishonest personal financial gain
  • Machinery Breakdown

  • Glass

    • Damage to frames, burglar alarm strips, wires and sensor vibrators
    • Cost of removal and reinstallation of fixtures and fittings necessary for the replacement of the glass
    • Cost of employment of a security service prior to replacement of glass unless payable under any other insurance arranged by the Insured or tenant
